## The classic stops

From Hue, heading south:

1. **Lang Co beach** — lunch stop, empty white-sand crescent.
2. **Hai Van Pass** — the 21-km ridge, 500 m above the sea. Pull off at the old French bunker for the photo.
3. **Lap An lagoon** — shallow mirror-calm bay, oyster farms.
4. **Marble Mountains** — 20-min climb, caves and pagodas.
5. **Lady Buddha (Linh Ung Pagoda)** — sunset view over Da Nang.
6. Drop-off in Hoi An around 6pm.
