## At a glance

| Day | Location | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hanoi | Arrive, Old Quarter |
| 2 | Hanoi | Full city day |
| 3 | Ninh Binh | Transfer, Trang An |
| 4 | Ninh Binh → Hanoi → Ha Long | Sunrise Mua Cave, fly Ha Long late afternoon* |
| 5 | Ha Long cruise | Overnight on boat |
| 6 | Ha Long → Hanoi → fly Da Nang → Hoi An | Long transfer day |
| 7 | Hoi An | Old Town |
| 8 | Hoi An | Beach or My Son sunrise |
| 9 | Fly to HCMC | Afternoon in District 1 |
| 10 | HCMC | War Remnants Museum, Cu Chi half day, fly home |

*Book cruise pickup for day 5 instead and spend day 4 afternoon in Hanoi if you prefer a calmer pace.
