## The structure

- **Days 1–2** Hanoi.
- **Days 3–4** Sapa (overnight bus up, day trek, overnight bus back).
- **Days 5–6** Ninh Binh.
- **Days 7–8** Ha Long Bay overnight cruise.
- **Day 9** Fly Hanoi → Hue.
- **Day 10** Hue + Hai Van Pass to Hoi An.
- **Days 11–12** Hoi An.
- **Day 13** Fly to Ho Chi Minh City.
- **Day 14** Mekong Delta full day then fly out.
